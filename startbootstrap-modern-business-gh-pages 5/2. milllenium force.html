      Roller coaster in Sandusky, Ohio
      Millennium Force is a steel roller coaster built by Intamin at Cedar Point amusement park in Sandusky, Ohio, United States. It was the fourteenth roller coaster to be built at the park since Blue Streak opened in 1964. Wikipedia
      Address: 1 Frontier Town Dr, Sandusky, OH 44870
      Height: 308
      Max speed: 93mph
      Height restriction: 3 11
      G-force: 4.5g
      Track length: 6,595
      Phone: (419) 627-2350
      Did you know: Although Millennium Force has been surpassed in height and speed, it remains one of the tallest and fastest in the world. wikipedia.org
      Website: https://www.cedarpoint.com/things-to-do/roller-coasters
